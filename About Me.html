About Me

I had been practicing Yoga for 5 years before I completed my Yoga Teacher Training Course in India. My decision to make the shift from IT to Yoga was driven, as cliché as it sounds, by a desire to understand what I was doing, or as most Yogi’s put it “to deepen my practice.”

I use Yoga to settle my mind and connect with the present moment and my body. My practice attempts to explore emotional blockages held within the body and explore ways of releasing these.

My practice has been influenced by my interests within counselling and psychotherapy. I am currently studying to gain my Post-Graduate Dipolma in Counselling and Psychotherapy. Upon completion I will then begin to offer a thorough holistic approach to body and mind.
