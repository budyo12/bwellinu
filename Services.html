Offerings

I provide one-to-one, group and corporate Vinyasa sessions. We will discuss your needs and goals before we start any classes. This will give us both an idea of what to expect and what to focus on. This could be building strength, improving flexibility, feeling more energised and calmer.

Private sessions are a great way to introduce yourself to yoga and to tie in with your schedule. Group sessions are a great way to socialise and have fun with friends. Corporate sessions provide a great benefit to employees by targeting areas that are specific to office related aches and pains.
